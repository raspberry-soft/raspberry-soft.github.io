A lighthearted visual novel based on the concept of Netorase (Wife Sharing). Hana is a loving and understanding wife that realizes her husband fetish, and while Yuma, a young man, is staying at their home during college, she takes matters into her own hands. Her husband, our protagonist, anxious at first, realizes the pleasure of sharing his wife.

Hana: A loving and bold housewife. She wants to make her husband happy at all costs. She loves to tease him and is full of surprises.

Yuma: A young man staying at the household. He is inexperienced with women.

Tatsuki: Our protagonist. A salaryman working hard to give his wife a comfortable life. He gets excited by the idea of sharing his wife.

Katsuo: An angry neighbor with unclear intentions.
